The spine. The board was unanimous on all 228 votes over two years. By its Vice Chair's own account, the deliberation that would explain that happens in an hour-long executive session before each public meeting — and a media-law attorney says the Sunshine Act does not permit a closed session that decides the day's agenda. The dispute is not whether the board agrees. It is where the deciding happens, and whether the public is allowed to watch.

Unquestioned: 156, not 142
