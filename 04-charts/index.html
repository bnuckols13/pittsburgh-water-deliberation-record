Two years of the Pittsburgh Water board, in five charts

Every resolution the board voted, October 2023 to September 2025, coded from the official minutes. Figures reproduce from the archived data with python 03-harness/build.py. Reporting by Brian Nuckols for Public Source.

Source: Pittsburgh Water board minutes (24 meetings, Oct 2023–Sep 2025), coded by the reporter; peer minutes from the Niagara Falls Water Board and North Harris County Regional Water Authority.
